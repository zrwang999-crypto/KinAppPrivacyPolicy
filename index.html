1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Kin App ("Kin", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, protects and otherwise processes your personal information when you access or use Kin App, including our mobile application, related pages, content, community features and other services that link to this Policy.

Kin App is a community content and social product. Current features may include account registration and login through Apple, Google or Facebook, invitation code verification, profile creation, browsing and publishing posts, images or videos, following or connecting with other users, private messages, likes, comments, saves, shares, reports, recommended feeds, subscriptions, notifications and account settings.

If a specific feature is not available in your version of Kin App, the related data processing will not apply until that feature is enabled.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information only where it is necessary for providing, securing, improving or complying with legal obligations related to Kin App.

2.1 Account and Login Information

When you register, log in or verify your account, we may collect your third-party login identifier, invitation code, account ID, login status and authentication records.

If you choose to log in through Apple, Google, Facebook or another supported third-party service, we may receive limited information authorized by you or provided by that service, such as your third-party account identifier, display name, email address or profile image.

2.2 Profile Information

You may provide profile information such as avatar, nickname, bio, gender, interests, links, location shown on your profile, profile background or other information you choose to make visible.

2.3 Content and Community Information

When you use Kin App's community features, we may collect the content you create, upload, publish, comment on, save, like, share, report or send in private messages, including text, images, videos, audio, captions, hashtags, links, metadata, drafts, comment records, private message records, interaction records and moderation records.

Your public content, public profile information and public interactions may be visible to other users according to your settings and the product design.

2.4 Device, Log and Usage Information

To operate and secure Kin App, we may collect device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, network type, IP address, device identifiers, crash logs, diagnostic information, access time, pages viewed, feature clicks, referral source and other usage logs.

2.5 Media, Camera and Album Permissions

If you choose to upload or publish images or videos, Kin App may request access to your camera, photo library or file storage. We only access the media you select or create through Kin App, unless your device operating system grants broader permission and you choose to allow it.

You may disable permissions through your device settings. Some publishing or upload features may not work after permission is disabled.

2.6 Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we may process device push tokens and notification preferences to send account alerts, interaction reminders, community updates and service notices. You can disable notifications in Kin App or your device settings.

2.7 Customer Support, Reports and Safety Information

When you contact us, submit feedback, report content or appeal a decision, we may collect your contact details, report category, description, screenshots, related content, user IDs, processing records and communications with us.

2.8 Payment and Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription or other paid feature, payment may be processed through Apple Pay, Google Pay, Apple App Store, Google Play or other payment channels made available in Kin App. We may receive transaction identifiers, purchase status, subscription status, product type, payment time, renewal/cancellation status and other information necessary to provide paid services, verify purchases, prevent fraud and handle customer support. We do not receive your full payment card number from Apple Pay or Google Pay.

2.9 Information From Third Parties

Where permitted by law and your authorization, we may receive information from third-party login providers, analytics providers, security service providers, cloud hosting providers, app stores, payment providers or other partners necessary to provide Kin App. Current third-party services may include Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook Sign In, Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, push notification services, cloud storage services, content moderation services, Google Pay and Apple Pay.

3. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

  1. Create, verify, manage and secure your account.
  2. Provide core features, including browsing, publishing, searching, recommending, private messaging, liking, commenting, saving, sharing, reporting, subscription management and account settings.
  3. Display your profile, public content and interactions to other users.
  4. Personalize feeds, content recommendations, language, layout and user experience.
  5. Send service notices, security alerts, interaction reminders and optional marketing messages where permitted.
  6. Review reported content, prevent fraud, spam, abuse, illegal content, account misuse and security incidents.
  7. Analyze performance, fix bugs, improve product design and develop new features.
  8. Protect the rights, safety and property of users, Kin App and third parties.
  9. Comply with legal obligations, regulatory requirements, dispute resolution and enforcement of our Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Kin App and related web pages may use cookies, SDKs, local storage, device identifiers or similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, analyze usage, secure the service and improve performance.

You may manage cookies through browser or device settings, but some functions may not work properly if cookies or identifiers are disabled.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or disclose information in the following limited situations:

  1. With your consent or at your direction.
  2. With service providers who support hosting, storage, analytics, push notifications, security, customer support, payment processing, content moderation or other operational needs.
  3. With third-party login or sharing services when you choose to use them.
  4. To other users, when information is part of your public profile, public content or visible interactions.
  5. To comply with laws, regulations, court orders, government requests or law enforcement requirements.
  6. To protect rights, safety, property, security and prevent harm or illegal activity.
  7. In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, transfer of assets or similar transaction, subject to appropriate protection measures.

We require service providers to process information only for authorized purposes and to apply reasonable security safeguards.

6. International Transfer

Kin App currently stores and processes data primarily in Los Angeles, United States. Depending on our operations and service providers, your information may also be processed in other countries or regions outside your location. Where required by applicable law, we will use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers, such as contractual protections, security assessments or other legally recognized mechanisms.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for the shortest period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

When your account is deleted or information is no longer necessary, we will delete, anonymize or otherwise handle it according to applicable law. Some information may remain for a limited period in backups, security logs, dispute records or legal compliance records.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, copy, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal information, withdraw consent, manage permissions, opt out of certain messages, request account deletion or lodge a complaint with a regulator.

You can exercise some rights through Kin App's account settings. For other requests, contact us at [privacy@kinapp.example]. We may verify your identity before processing your request.

9. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Kin App account through Me -> Setting -> Account security -> Delete account. After deletion, we will stop providing account-based services and delete or anonymize your personal information unless retention is required by law, security, dispute resolution or legitimate business needs.

Deleted public content may not be recoverable. Content already copied, cached, shared by others or stored by third-party services may remain outside our control.

10. Children and Minors

Kin App is not intended for minors. You must be at least 18 years old, or the age of majority in your jurisdiction if higher, to use Kin App.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a minor who is not permitted to use Kin App, we will delete it or take other appropriate measures as required by law.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss and misuse. However, no internet service is completely secure. Please protect your account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

12. Third-Party Services

Kin App may contain links to third-party websites, apps, social platforms or sharing tools. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We are not responsible for third-party services that we do not control.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide notice through Kin App, website notice, email, push notification or other reasonable means. The updated Policy becomes effective on the date stated above unless otherwise specified.
